# Members

Manage who has access to your organization's projects and memories.

## Roles

- **Owner** — Full control, including billing and org deletion
- **Admin** — Can manage projects, members, and tokens
- **Member** — Can view projects and use MCP tools

## Member Limits

| Plan | Members |
|---|---|
| Free | 2 |
| Lite | 5 |
| Pro | 15 |
| Business | 50 |
| Better | 200 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |
