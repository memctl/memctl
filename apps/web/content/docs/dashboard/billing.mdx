# Billing

mem/ctl uses Stripe for billing. Subscriptions are organization-based.

## Plans

| Plan | Price/mo | Projects | Members | Memories/project | API calls/mo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 2 | 2 | 1,000 | 10,000 |
| Lite | $5 | 5 | 5 | 5,000 | 50,000 |
| Pro | $20 | 20 | 15 | 25,000 | 250,000 |
| Business | $40 | 50 | 50 | 100,000 | 1,000,000 |
| Better | $80 | 200 | 200 | 500,000 | 5,000,000 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## Upgrading

Click "Upgrade" in your billing settings. You'll be redirected to Stripe Checkout. After payment, your plan limits are updated immediately.

## Managing Subscription

Click "Manage subscription" to access the Stripe customer portal, where you can:

- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Change or cancel plan
